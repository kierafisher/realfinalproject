Lessons


Discover the joy of playing the harp with personalized private music lessons tailored to your unique goals and abilities. Whether you’re a complete beginner or an experienced musician looking to refine your skills, my lessons provide a supportive and inspiring environment to help you succeed. With 15 years of harp experience and a passion for teaching, I focus on building a strong foundation in technique while nurturing your creativity and love for music. Lessons are designed to fit your learning style, ensuring progress at a comfortable and rewarding pace. In my private studio, you’ll explore a diverse repertoire of music, from classical and traditional pieces to contemporary arrangements. I incorporate music theory, ear training, and performance practice into lessons, providing a well-rounded education that helps you grow as a musician. Whether you dream of playing for personal enjoyment, performing for an audience, or pursuing a career in music, I’ll guide you every step of the way. For students interested in creative expression, I also offer opportunities to develop improvisation and composition skills. As a dedicated teacher, I strive to make every lesson engaging and fulfilling. My goal is to inspire confidence and cultivate a lifelong love of music in my students. Lessons are available for all ages and skill levels, with flexible scheduling to accommodate your busy life. Whether you’re picking up the harp for the first time or returning to music after a break, I’m here to help you reach your full potential. Contact me today to start your harp journey and bring the magic of music into your life!
